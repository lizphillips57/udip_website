    Merchandise

    Want some cool UCSB physics department merch? UDIP has you covered (literally)! Check this page to see what we currently have in stock! Restocks will be periodically announced on the Physics Discord throughout the year.

    How to order

    Spring quarter 23: We will be selling merch every Monday, 1-2 pm in the Broida Lobby. For any inquiries about merch, please contact physics-udip@ucsb.edu.

    You can also send us a message in the Physics Discord server, under channel name #udip-general-chat, and we'll be sure to help you out.
