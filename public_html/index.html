    Undergraduate Diversity and Inclusion in Physics

    UDIP is a club committed to creating a welcoming environment within the physics department at UCSB. We work with the department and coordinate with faculty, students, and other clubs to put on great events and provide resources.

    Broida Welcome Tour 2022!

    Hello Physics Students!

    The UCSB Physics Department in collaboration with UDIP and Astronomy Society is inviting you all to our Broida Welcome Tour on Saturday, September 24! This is an opportunity to meet your fellow physics students, get to know Broida Hall, tour some labs, and see many of the resources available to you.

    The event will consist of three parts:
    3:30 - 6:00: Lab tours, Tabling, and Scavenger hunt
    6:00 - 7:00: Dinner
    7:00 - 9:00: Astronomy Night

    The following labs will holding tours: Young Lab, Bouwmeester Lab, Mazin Lab, Fygenson Lab, Weld Lab, Moody Lab, Streichan Lab, Jayich Lab.

    Please fill out this form to sign up for lab tours and give us an estimate of attendance!
