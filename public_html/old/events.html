    November 10th, 6-7pm PST

    Do you want to apply for an internship, scholarship, or other program that requires a Statement of Purpose - and you have no idea where to start? Join us at this workshop, where we will break down the Statement of Purpose (why are they even required? what should be in them?) and get you started writing your own. We will focus on strategies for breaking through writer’s block, organizing your thoughts, and composing effective statements that highlight your strengths and relevant skills. We will also discuss some of the research opportunities at UCSB!
